WomenKind Mobile App — Feature Brainstorm

The core SMS check-in flow is locked in. Pick any additional features that resonate — we'll use this to scope the app.

Core (already decided)
📱
Weekly SMS → Deep Link → Check-in

Text arrives Monday morning. Tap link. App opens to check-in. Done in 60 seconds.

IN SCOPE
Health Tracking
A

Wearable Sync (Apple Health / Oura)

Auto-fills sleep & energy from your ring or watch. Check-in asks fewer questions.

B

Symptom Photo Log

Quick photo attachments to a symptom entry — e.g. hot flash rash, bloating. Visible to provider.

C

Daily Mood / Notes Journal

Free-text or voice memo attached to a day's entry. Private or shareable with provider.

D

Cycle Tracking

Log period days and flow. Correlates with symptom spikes in the trend chart.

Medication & Treatment
A

Medication Reminders

Push notification at a set time each day: "Take your estradiol." Tap to log it as taken.

B

One-Tap Refill Request

App surfaces "Refill due soon" banner. One tap sends the request to the provider.

C

Prescription Timeline

Visual timeline of when medications started, changed dose, or were discontinued.

Care & Communication
A

Secure Messaging (In-App)

Message Dr. Urban directly. Supports text and image attachments. Push notifications for replies.

B

Visit Prep Checklist

Before your appointment: "Here's what to bring up based on your recent check-ins."

C

After-Visit Summary

AI-generated note after each visit: what was discussed, next steps, treatment changes.

D

Lab Results Push Alert

"Your labs are ready." Tap to view results with plain-English interpretation.

Progress & Motivation
A

Check-in Streak

Streak counter + badge for consistent tracking. Shown on the home screen.

B

Monthly Progress Report

Auto-generated PDF-style recap: score change, best domain, worst domain, milestones.

C

Wins Board

Surfaced moments like "Sleep improved 30% this month" or "Longest hot-flash-free streak."

D

Health Blueprint (In-App)

Your personalized treatment overview — readable anytime, not buried in onboarding.

Scheduling
A

In-App Appointment Booking

Book, reschedule, or cancel visits. Calendar integration (add to Apple/Google cal).

B

Appointment Reminders

Push at 24h and 1h before. Includes join-video-call button for telehealth visits.
